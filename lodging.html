Getting to Greenport

By Ferry

The Cross Sound Ferry runs regularly between New London, CT and Orient Point, NY. If you are coming from upstate New York or somewhere in New England, this is the best option. Orient Point is a scenic fifteen minute drive from Greenport.

  • Ferry operator: Cross Sound Ferry
  • Duration: 1 hour 20 minutes
  • Reservations are recommended

By Plane

We recommend flying into either LaGuardia or the Long Island MacArthur airport.

From LaGuardia, you can:

  • Rent a car and drive to Greenport
  • Long Island Railroad
    • Take an uber or taxi to the Long Island Railroad Woodside stop. Take train headed towards Ronkonkoma, transfer at Ronkonkoma station for train to Greenport
    • The train from Ronkonkoma to Greenport runs infrequently, so check the time table
  • Jitney Bus
    • Take bus or taxi to Queens Airport Connection stop in Fresh Meadows, Queens. The Jitney stops in the center of Greenport

From Long Island MacArthur airport, you can:

  • Rent a car and drive to Greenport

From NYC

  • Long Island Rail Road (LIRR) from Penn Station to Greenport
    • Route: Take the Ronkonkoma line and transfer at Ronkonkoma for the Greenport train
    • Duration: About 3 hours
    • The Greenport station is in the center of town
  • Jitney Bus
